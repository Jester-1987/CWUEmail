With that in mind, please allow us to give you a brief tour of our app that has been specially designed to make it as easy as possible to find the perfect caregiver match for you.

Step One:

Download our app onto your mobile device. It's available for both Apple and Android devices.

  

Step Two:

Once you open the app on your mobile device, you will be asked to sign in with your e-mail address and password.


Step Three:

Choose whether the care is for yourself or a loved one. We also work with clients/residents who are living in care facilities.


Step Four:

Choose the type of care you would like to recieve. We offer a wide range of services; see this section of our app for an entire list. You can choose as many or as few as you need.


Step Five:

After choosing the type of care you like to recive, you can also specify how much or how little care you could like to recieve. You can also describe the task you need in more detail.

Now there's only one more step to go!


Step Six:

Our app will now show you a list of all the qualified caregivers who are available to help. You can tap on their profiles to schedule a visit.

For your safety, we ensure on our end that all caregivers have the necessary qualifications and are screened through security checks.


If you have any questions or need assistance, our team is here to help! Feel free to contact us anytime.
